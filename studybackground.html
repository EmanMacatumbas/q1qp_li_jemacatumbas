Context

Depression is a mental state wherein a person will experience a persistent feeling of unmotivation or sadness. Depression rates and number of cases have been on the rise in the past years. More and more People have shown to exhibit signs of this mental condition across the world. This could negatively affect our society as people will become less motivated or willing to work due to their condition. This could potentially halt the progress and development of a country, society, community, and even each indiviual person.

Goal of the Study

The goal of this study is to gather and analyze the data we have gathered from a sample of the top 50 countries with the highest number cases of Depression to achieve two things:

  1. Understand the trends of depression and infer possible factors and reasons for such rates
  2. Raise awareness of these rates towards a general audience and assist future researcher formulate possible solutions to the issue.
